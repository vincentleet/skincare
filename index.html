⚠ Restart Context

Your skin barrier was compromised by an allergic reaction. You're mostly recovered but still slightly sensitive. The retinol and salicylic acid schedules restart from Week 1 — don't rush them. The new products have been integrated in a way that supports barrier recovery first, then active performance.

Stock note: ~12ml Glow Deep Serum · ~15ml Hyaluronic Acid · ~30ml Retinol 0.2% · ~8ml Salicylic Acid 2%

Why These Products Work Together

Skin1004 Centella Ampoule

Madecassoside + asiaticoside = the two compounds in centella with the most evidence for barrier repair and redness reduction. The "tone brightening" element comes from niacinamide and tranexamic acid. Excellent post-barrier-break addition. Use morning and Wednesday PM.

BoJ Calming Serum (Green Tea + Panthenol)

Panthenol (B5) is a humectant that also accelerates wound healing — it's unusually good paired with actives. Green tea's EGCG is a proven antioxidant. Pairing this after SA nights is genuinely smart skincare, not just filler. Also perfect for post-retinol recovery (Friday).

BoJ Revive Serum (Ginseng + Snail Mucin)

Snail secretion filtrate has decent evidence for pore appearance, skin texture smoothing, and mild collagen stimulation. Ginseng is your collagen/firming ingredient. This is the "weird but actually works" product in your lineup — it earns its Sunday slot and eventually the daily AM slot.

BoJ Radiance Cleansing Balm

Oil-based first cleanse is the correct method for dissolving SPF and sebum without disrupting the barrier. Your previous routine skipped this step — which means some SPF residue was likely sitting on skin all night, reducing the efficacy of everything applied after. This fixes that.

BoJ Ginseng Essence Water

Replaces the Saccharomyces toner in both AM and PM. Slightly more active — fermented ginseng + niacinamide combo addresses brightness and early signs of aging simultaneously. The "essence" texture absorbs faster and prepares skin better for serums than a standard toner.

BoJ Dynasty Cream

Morning moisturiser upgrade. Contains ginseng root extract, niacinamide, and a lipid-rich base that's closer to your skin's natural structure than CeraVe. Better synergy with the rest of your K-beauty lineup. CeraVe stays in the evenings — ceramides excel at barrier-sealing after actives.

Realistic Expectations

Wks 1–2
Barrier feels more stable. Less post-shave sensitivity. Skin looks calmer. Centella starts reducing redness.
Wks 2–4
Texture improves noticeably. Pores look less inflamed. Ginseng Essence Water starts building a glow. Snail mucin smooths surface.
Wks 4–8
SA keeps pores clear. Retinol begins real texture work — fine lines around nose reduce. Centella tone brightening becomes visible.
Wks 8–12
Assess retinol increase. Skin looks more "polished." Pore depth visibly reduced. Snail mucin has had time to work on any acne marks.
12+ wks
This is when the full stack pays off. The combination of retinol (cell turnover), SA (pore clearing), snail mucin (texture), and ginseng (collagen) is genuinely comprehensive for your concerns.
Skin laxity
Honest note: the "tighter skin" feeling you're after relates to subcutaneous structure. Topicals cannot change fat distribution. Retinol + ginseng + snail mucin will improve skin quality and firmness at the surface level — which is visible and worthwhile — but for deeper laxity, that's beyond what any routine can address.

Adjustment Rules

✅ Green lights — can increase retinol to 2×/week

○No stinging on application
○No peeling or flaking
○No lingering redness after shaving
○Skin feels normal the next morning
○Moisturiser feels "enough"

❌ Red flags — do not increase yet

○Burning or warmth after retinol
○Tightness lasting into next morning
○Flakes around nose or mouth
○Shaving suddenly stings more
○Skin looks shiny but feels sore
General Rules

If skin stings or flakes → skip retinol for 1–2 weeks and lean on Calming Serum + CeraVe only.

If SA runs out and pores worsen → repurchase, or consider Paula's Choice 2% BHA as a longer-term option (more economical per ml).

Never use retinol and SA on the same night. The current schedule keeps them separated correctly.

Vitamin C and retinol are not in this routine — if you want to add a Vitamin C serum in future, it belongs in the morning before SPF and pairs well with the Centella ampoule.
